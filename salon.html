About Glow Kathmandu

Hair-First. Always.

We are a unisex hair salon based in the heart of Thamel, Kathmandu. Whether you are a man wanting a clean fade and beard trim, or a woman looking for a bold balayage and a fresh cut — our team of trained stylists is here for you.

We believe great hair is not about gender. It is about skill, the right products, and a stylist who truly listens.

About Glow Kathmandu

Hair-First.
Always.

We are a unisex hair salon in the heart of Thamel, Kathmandu. Whether you are a man after a clean fade and beard trim, or a woman wanting bold balayage and a fresh cut — our trained stylists are here for you.

We believe great hair has nothing to do with gender. It is about skill, the right products, and a stylist who truly listens.

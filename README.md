## 🚀 How to Use

### For Clients
1. Visit the salon website on your phone
2. Click "Book Now" - the wizard will guide you through:
   - **Service Selection**: Choose from 6 popular services with prices
   - **Barber Choice**: Pick your preferred stylist or "Any Available"
   - **Date & Time**: Select from available slots (shows booked times)
   - **Contact Details**: Enter name and WhatsApp number
   - **Confirmation**: Review and confirm your booking

### For Barbers/Admins
1. Open `admin.html` in your browser
2. **Dashboard**: See metrics, recent bookings, today's schedule
3. **Today's Schedule**: View all appointments with one-click WhatsApp
4. **All Bookings**: Filter and manage all appointments
5. **Clients**: Track client history and preferences
6. **Revenue**: Monitor business performance
